Our Services

AZ Aluminium was founded to play a leading role in the aluminum sector. The company offers a wide range of solutions that meet customers' needs in the design and construction of aluminum structures.

It specializes in creating winter gardens (verandas) and pergolas, including bioclimatic systems which provide beautiful and comfortable spaces for various applications.

We proudly operate across Germany, Belgium, and the Netherlands, delivering high-quality innovative and sustainable solutions that make us the ideal choice for modern design.
