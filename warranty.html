Warranty

Built to last — backed by our comprehensive warranty coverage for complete peace of mind.

Our Commitment to Quality

Every AZ Aluminium product is engineered and manufactured to the highest European standards. We stand behind the quality of our materials and workmanship, which is why we offer some of the most comprehensive warranties in the industry.

Our warranties cover manufacturing defects and material failures, ensuring your investment is protected for years to come. Terms and conditions apply depending on the product type and installation method.

Important: Warranty coverage is valid only for products installed by AZ Aluminium certified installers or authorised partners. Unauthorised installation may void the warranty.

Warranty Terms

Understand the full scope of your warranty coverage

Warranty Period

Warranty coverage begins on the date of installation or product delivery (whichever is later). The specific duration depends on the product type as outlined above.

Transferability

Warranty is transferable to new property owners within the warranty period. Proof of original purchase and installation documentation is required.

What Is Covered

Manufacturing defects in aluminium profiles, surface coating, hardware (hinges, locks, handles), and motorised components. Defects must be reported within 14 days of discovery.

Claim Requirements

Valid proof of purchase, installation certificate, and photographic evidence of the defect are required. AZ Aluminium reserves the right to inspect the product before approving a claim.

Resolution Timeline

We aim to acknowledge warranty claims within 48 hours and resolve or replace defective items within 30 working days, subject to product availability and assessment.

What Is Not Covered

  • Damage from improper use, misuse, or abuse
  • Normal wear and tear, scratches, minor marks
  • External factor damage — storms, floods, fire, vandalism
  • Non-certified or unauthorised installation
  • Glass breakage (separate manufacturer warranty)
  • Colour variation from ageing or environmental exposure
  • Failure to follow maintenance guidelines
  • Unauthorised third-party modifications or repairs
