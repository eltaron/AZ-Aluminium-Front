Our Services

Engineering aluminium excellence since our founding — serving Germany, Belgium, and the Netherlands.

AZ Aluminium was founded to play a leading role in the aluminum sector. The company offers a wide range of solutions that meet customers' needs in the design and construction of aluminum structures. It specializes in creating winter gardens (verandas) and pergolas, including bioclimatic pergolas, which provide beautiful and comfortable spaces for various applications.

AZ Aluminium also offers a range of doors, including garage doors and automatic doors equipped with advanced technologies to ensure safety and comfort. Additionally, their services include the manufacturing and installation of roller shutters, allowing customers to enjoy suitable lighting and privacy in their homes.

The company operates in three countries: Germany, Belgium, and the Netherlands, enabling it to deliver high-quality services and meet customer expectations in different markets. AZ Aluminium aims to provide innovative and sustainable solutions, making it the ideal choice for anyone seeking high-quality aluminum products with a modern design.
