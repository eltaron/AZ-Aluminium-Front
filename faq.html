Products & Systems

Warranty & Support

We offer a 10-year structural warranty on the aluminium frame and a 5-year warranty{' '} on motors and moving parts. This covers manufacturing defects and material faults under normal use.

Aluminium requires minimal maintenance. We recommend cleaning the frames with mild soap and water twice a year. For motorized systems, the motors should be serviced every 2–3 years by a certified technician.

Contact our support team immediately. If the issue is covered under warranty, we will repair or replace the defective part free of charge. For non-warranty issues, we offer competitive repair services with quick turnaround.

We operate in Germany, Belgium, and the Netherlands. Our team serves both residential and commercial clients across these markets with local support and installation services.
