Maintenance Guide

Expert care instructions to keep your aluminium systems looking pristine and performing at their best for decades.

Product Care Guides

Select a product category for detailed maintenance instructions

Cleaning
  • Wipe frames with mild soap and warm water using a soft cloth
  • Rinse thoroughly with clean water — avoid harsh chemicals
  • Clean glass with a standard glass cleaner and lint-free cloth
  • Dry all surfaces to prevent water spots
Lubrication
  • Apply silicone-based lubricant to hinges and tracks twice a year
  • Avoid oil-based lubricants that attract dust
  • Wipe excess lubricant after application
  • Check and lubricate locking mechanisms annually
Inspection
  • Check weather seals for cracks or wear every 6 months
  • Test all moving parts for smooth operation
  • Inspect drainage channels for blockages
  • Tighten any loose screws or fittings
Common Issues & Fixes
  • Sticky operation → clean tracks and apply silicone lubricant
  • Drafts → inspect and replace worn weather seals
  • Condensation → check drainage and improve ventilation
  • Loose handle → tighten internal fixing screws
Frame & Louvres
  • Clean aluminium profiles with mild detergent and water
  • Remove debris from louvre drainage tracks
  • Check louvre alignment and adjust if needed
  • Inspect all bolts and brackets for tightness
Motor & Electronics
  • Test motorised operation monthly
  • Keep control unit dry and free from dust
  • Replace remote batteries annually
  • Check wind/rain sensor function before storm seasons
Winter Care
  • Clear snow accumulation from louvres promptly
  • Check for ice buildup in drainage channels
  • Store removable cushions and accessories indoors
  • Ensure LED lighting seals are intact
Annual Service
  • Schedule a professional inspection yearly
  • Lubricate all pivot points and moving joints
  • Tighten all structural connections
  • Update firmware for smart pergola systems
Glass Care
  • Clean glass with non-abrasive cleaners and soft cloth
  • Avoid hard water — use deionised water for streak-free finish
  • Check for chips or cracks after storms
  • Keep glass edge seals clean and dry
Sliding Mechanism
  • Keep bottom tracks clean from dirt and grit
  • Apply silicone lubricant to rollers annually
  • Check roller alignment for smooth gliding
  • Adjust roller height if door drags
Seals & Weatherproofing
  • Inspect perimeter seals for wear every 6 months
  • Replace damaged brush seals promptly
  • Check drainage weep holes are clear
  • Test water tightness after heavy rain
Locking Systems
  • Test multi-point locks regularly
  • Lubricate lock mechanism with graphite powder
  • Check that all locking points engage fully
  • Tighten any loose strike plates
Slats & Guides
  • Clean slats with a damp cloth and mild detergent
  • Keep side guide tracks free of debris
  • Check for bent or damaged slats after storms
  • Ensure bottom bar is level and secure
Motor & Controls
  • Test motor operation weekly during heavy use periods
  • Listen for unusual noises during operation
  • Keep control switches clean and dry
  • Check emergency manual override function
Belt & Spring Tension
  • Check belt tension every 6 months
  • Listen for clicking or slipping sounds
  • Adjust spring tension if shutter drops under its own weight
  • Professional servicing recommended every 2 years
Seasonal Checks
  • Check drainage at bottom of shutter box
  • Remove ice buildup gently — never force operation
  • Inspect housing for water ingress after heavy rain
  • Test photocell sensors for safety function

Seasonal Maintenance Checklist

Stay on top of your product care throughout the year

Spring
  • Clean all tracks and guides
  • Test motorised systems
  • Check seals after winter
  • Lubricate moving parts
Summer
  • Clean glass surfaces regularly
  • Check insect screens
  • Test pergola louvre operation
  • Inspect external fittings
Autumn
  • Clear leaves from drainage
  • Check weather seals
  • Prepare for winter storms
  • Test wind sensors
Winter
  • Remove snow from louvres
  • Check for ice damage
  • Avoid forcing frozen parts
  • Monitor condensation levels

Pro Tips for Long-Lasting Performance

Simple habits that extend the life of your aluminium systems

1
Use the Right Cleaning Products

Never use abrasive cleaners, bleach, or acidic products on aluminium surfaces. Stick to pH-neutral detergents and soft microfiber cloths to protect the anodised or powder-coated finish.

2
Catch Issues Early

A small squeak or stiff operation today is a bigger problem tomorrow. Address minor issues as soon as you notice them — a drop of lubricant now can save a costly repair later.

3
Don't Force Stuck Parts

If a door, window, or shutter becomes difficult to operate, don't force it. Check for obstructions in tracks, inspect seals, and contact our support team if the issue persists.

4
Keep Documentation Handy

Save your product manuals, warranty certificates, and maintenance records. They contain model-specific care instructions and will be useful for future servicing or warranty claims.

5
Schedule Professional Servicing

While regular DIY care is essential, we recommend a professional inspection every 2 years to keep motorised systems, seals, and structural fittings in optimal condition.
